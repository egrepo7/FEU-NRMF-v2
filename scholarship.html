Since June 2013, The International Society has continued to encourage students of the FEU-NRMF School of Medical Technology to excel by providing scholarship grants to deserving students. Each application is reviewd by the Phillipine Committee and presented to our Chair of Scholarship Program for approval. Grants are provided for students going to Fourth Year or Internships. Four grants of $300 per semester per scholar are available.

Excellence Awards

The Excellence Awards started in 2011 with two subjects. In 2012, the program added 3 more subjects plus the President's Award for Most Outstanding Intern. In 2013, it has expanded further to 9 subjects. The Awards are presented to the deserving students at the Annual Alumni Homecoming sponsored by the FEU-NRMF MT Alumni Society in the Philippines. All donors are members of the FEU-NRMF Medical Technology International Alumni Society. Winners receive a Certificate and $50 prize per subject. The Most Outstanding Intern gets a Certificate and a $100 prize.
