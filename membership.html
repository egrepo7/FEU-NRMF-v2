Membership

Thank you for your interest in becoming a member of our society.

Membership is open to all graduates of the FEU-NRMF School of Medical Technology. Good standing Philippine members of the FEU-NRMF MT Alumni Society are automatic honorary members of the FEU-NRMF MT International Alumni Society.

How to Apply

  1. Download membership form and mail with check to the following:
    Marieta Fernando Cajayon
    4120 Campobello Place
    Richmond, BC V7E 5G5 CANADA
  2. Register using the form below and mailing your check to:
    Marieta Fernando Cajayon
    4120 Campobello Place
    Richmond, BC V7E 5G5 CANADA

Payment Options

  • One Year- $30 (Renewal of membership in July of each year)
  • Two Years - $30 (Renewal of membership in July every 2 years)
  • Lifetime - $30 (Renewal of membership in July of each year)
